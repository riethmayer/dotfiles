# Survival Framework Reference

## The Stakes

**Reality check before every evaluation:**
- If we can't prove signals lead to meetings, EagleEye is abandoned
- Team is off the platform in Q1 while we fix the core
- This is existential, not optimization

## The Bet

We're betting on **people and networks**, not company coverage:
- Career transitions (high performers leaving strong companies)
- Pre-formation signals (before LinkedIn says "building something")
- Relationship graphs (who knows whom, warm paths)

## Time-to-Meeting Pipeline

```text
STAGE 1: Data → Signal      ← 2026 FOCUS
STAGE 2: Signal → Investor  ← 2026 FOCUS
STAGE 3: Investor → Outreach
STAGE 4: Outreach → Meeting
```

**Rule:** Stages 3-4 work is PAUSED until Stages 1-2 validated.

## Hard NOs (Automatic DANGER)

Do not spend time evaluating. Just flag and stop:
- Productivity tools (transcription, meeting prep, automation)
- Elaborate delivery UIs
- Bottom-up feature requests (in 2026)
- Competing with Harmonic on public company data

## Updated Playing to Win Cascade

1. **Win**: Prove signals create meetings (2026 survival)
2. **Play**: People/network intelligence where others have company data
3. **How**: Proprietary people data + network graphs + personalized delivery
4. **Capabilities**: Career signal detection, entity resolution, relationship mapping
5. **Systems**: Time-to-Meeting (full pipeline), RAT weighted scores, Moat accumulation

## RAT Survival Sequencing

RAT weighted scores optimize for learning. Survival requires **sequencing**:

| Priority | Assumption | Why First |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | 90% ER achievable | BLOCKING — enables all downstream |
| 2 | Data sources provide coverage | BLOCKING — no data = no signals |
| 3 | Career signals predict founder intent | CORE BET validation |
| 4 | Network data accessible | Enables relationship layer |
| 5 | Leading indicators change behavior | Stage 2 — wait for Stage 1 |

**Key:** RAT ranks #5 highest (83.4), but survival ranks it #5.

## Moat Accumulation Indicators

Work should compound into these moats:
- **Proprietary data**: Sources competitors don't have
- **Entity resolution**: Better matching = better signals
- **Network intelligence**: Relationship graphs others can't build
- **Compounding effects**: Each signal improves future signal quality
