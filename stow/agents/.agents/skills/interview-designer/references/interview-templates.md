# Interview Templates & Question Bank

## Question Templates by Assumption Type

### Workflow Assumptions
- "Walk me through your morning routine for checking deal flow."
- "Show me where you track interesting founders."
- "What's the first thing you do when you see a promising signal?"

### Preference Assumptions
- "Tell me about the last founder you reached out to. What made them stand out?"
- "What information do you wish you had that you don't?"
- "When you ignore a signal, what's usually the reason?"

### Trust Assumptions
- "How do you decide if a recommendation is worth your time?"
- "Tell me about a tool you stopped using. What happened?"
- "What would make you trust a system's recommendations?"

### Behavior Change Assumptions
- "How has your sourcing changed in the last year?"
- "What would need to be true for you to change how you find founders?"
- "Tell me about a new tool that actually changed your workflow."

## Question Mapping Examples

| Assumption | Bad Question | Good Question |
|------------|--------------|---------------|
| Investors want explanations | "Do you want explanations?" | "Walk me through the last signal you acted on. What made you trust it?" |
| Leading indicators change behavior | "Would leading indicators help?" | "Tell me about a deal you missed. When did you first hear about the founder?" |

## Interview Structure Template

```markdown
## Interview: [Assumption Set]

**Target:** [Who to interview]
**Duration:** [X minutes]
**Goal:** [What we're validating]

### Warm-up (2 min)
[Build rapport, explain purpose]

### Section 1: [Assumption A] (X min)
**Testing:** [A# - assumption text]

1. [Opening question - past behavior]
2. [Follow-up - specific example]
3. [Probe - why/why not]

**Listen for:** [What validates/invalidates]

### Section 2: [Assumption B] (X min)
...

### Wrap-up (2 min)
- "Anything else about how you find founders?"
- "If EagleEye could do one thing better?"
```

|---|----------|------------|

### Section 2: [Topic] (X min)
